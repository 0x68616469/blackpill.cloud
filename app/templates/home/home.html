Here is some of them :

NIP-05

NIP-05 is a human-readable identifier for your public key.
Your friends and followers will find your profile on Nostr easier & quicker.
It will also provide you with a verified checkmark on most of the Nostr clients.

Lightning Address forwarding

BlackPill's members can create a lightning address, so the payments sent to their custom BlackPill's lightning address will be forwarded to their existing Lightning Address.
They can also choose to use their NIP-05 ID, one address for both identity and payment.

Private relay

BlackPill's members are whitelisted on relay.blackpill.cloud !
A private relay is a great way to make sure that your notes are always available.

Media uploader

Upload, edit, and delete your photos and gifs quickly with our app.
Get a quick and short link to share with your followers on Nostr. Metadata will be automatically removed for privacy.

Key Converter

Convert a nostr public key to an old-style hex key !

Explore

Every member is listed on the explore section, making it easy to find other users to follow and talk with.
