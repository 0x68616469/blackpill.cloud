Relay - Documentation

"Relays are like the backend servers for Nostr. They allow Nostr clients to send them messages, and they may (or may not) store those messages and broadcast those messages to all other connected clients."

BlackPill offer you a private relay to send your notes and talk with others BlackPill's member!
A private relay is a great way to make sure that your notes are always available.

/!\ Every user will be whitelisted at midnight every day until we find a way to automate this.

Here are some information on the relay/server:
URL: wss://relay.blackpill.cloud
RAM: 16 gb
CPU: AMD Ryzen 5 1600X Six-Core Processor
SSD: 1 Tb
Save notes: Forever
