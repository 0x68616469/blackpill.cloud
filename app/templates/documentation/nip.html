NIP-05 - Documentation

NIP-05 is a human-readable identifier for your public key.
Your friends and followers will find your profile on Nostr easier & quicker and it will also provide you with a verified checkmark on most of the Nostr clients.

If you are a BlackPill's member, to set-up your BlackPill's NIP-05 go to your dashboard and click on "edit".

(INFO): Put your hex public key and not your nostr public key (npub), to convert those, go to the key converter tool.
