Lightning Address Forwarding - Documentation

BlackPill's members can create a lightning address, so the payments sent to their custom BlackPill's lightning address will be forwarded to their existing Lightning Address.
They can also choose to use their NIP-05 ID, one address for both identity and payment.

If you are a BlackPill's member, to set-up your BlackPill's lightning address go to your dashboard and click on "edit".
